## Sales Report

- **Objective:**
  - **1.** Create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)_
  - **2.** Conduct a comparison between _[market performance and sales target](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target.pdf)_
- **Purpose:** Empower the company to monitor and evaluate business sales and performance
- **Why analyze Sales Data?** To identify sales patterns and track KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)

## Financial Report

- **Objective:**
  - **1.** Create P&L (Profit & Loss) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)_ & _[Fiscal Month](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Month%20%26%20Quarters.pdf)_
  - **2.** Create P&L (Profit & Loss) reports for _[Markets](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20for%20Markets.pdf)_
  - **3.** Calculate _[GM% (Gross Margin %) by Quarters](https://github.com/ckanafie/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/GM%25%20by%20quarters(sub_zone).pdf)_
- **Purpose:** To evaluate financial performance, which helps in making decisions and to convey data and decisions with stakeholders
- **Why analyze Financial Data?** To help in competing with industry peers and compare with previous period performance for budgeting and forecasting
